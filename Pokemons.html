MY FAVOURITE POKEMONS

Home Page Pokemons About



Arbok is not a favourite pokemon for most Pokemon fans but I think it is a cool Pokemon even though it is on the side of the bad guys. As you can guess it is a poison-type snake-like Pokemon that is from Generation-1. It evolves from Ekans starting at level 22.



Scyther is probably a more likeable Pokemon than Arbok which is again from Generation-1. It is a bug-type Pokemon that evolves into Scizor when traded while holding a Metal Coat (from Generations 2 to 7). It also evolves into Kleavor as another final form when exposed to a Black Augurite.



Like Arbok, Dodrio is also probably not a favourite Pokemon for most people. However, I think with its three heads and ostrich-like look makes it a good looking Pokemon. It is also stronger than a lot of people know. Dodrio is a normal/flying-type Pokemon and it evolves from Dodue starting at level 31.



Articuno is one of the three legendary birds (others are being Zapdos and Moltres) from the Generation-1 as you all know. It has a really cool look with crystal blue color and its long tail. It is an extremely powerful ice/flying-type Pokemon. It also has a Galarian form that is a psychic/flying-type that has a purple-like color. Articuno is not known to evolve into or from any other Pokemon.



Wailord is the first Pokemon that I only know through games (and not watched) among the Pokemons that I mentioned so far. It is a huge whale-like Pokemon from Generation-3 that is also powerful and I think this makes it likeable. It evolves from Wailmer sarting at level 40.



Raikou is one of the three legendary beasts that resurrected by Ho-Oh after burning of the Brass Tower. Raikou is said to represent the lightning strike which ignited the fire that consumed the tower (More info here). It is an electric-type Pokemon with a tiger-like look and it is not known to evolve into or from any other Pokemon.



Seviper is another poison-type (from Gen-3) and snake-like Pokemon like Arbok and again it is not one favourite Pokemons for most people. And again I think it has a cool look with his different looking head, tooth and tail. Seviper is known his rivalry with another Pokemon called Zangoose.



Ninetales is a beautiful fire-type Pokemon from Generation-1. It evolves from another beautiful Pokemon Vulpix when exposed to a Fire Stone. Moreover, it has an Alolan form that is an ice/fairy-type Pokemon.



Onix is another powerful Pokemon from Generation-1. It is another snake-like Pokemon that I am sure has much more fans than the other ones. It is a rock/ground-type Pokemon that evolves into Steelix when traded while holding a Metal Coat (from Generations 2 to 7) or when exposed to a Metal Coat (Pokemon Legends: Arceus).



Rayquaza is a dragon/flying-type legendary Pokemon from Generation-3. Along with Kyogre and Groudon, Rayquaza is one of the super-ancient Pokemon,serving as the group's trio master. It can mega evolve into Mega Rayquaza which is extremely powerful if it knows Dragon Ascent.



I know that Dragonite had to be in this list. Most people probably became a fan of it after watching its mysterios debut as a giant one (Ep.13). Dragonite is a dragon/flying pseudo-legendary Pokemon from Generation-1 that is again extremely powerful. It evolves from Dragonair starting at level 55 and it is the final form of Dratini.


Giratina is a scary looking ghost/dragon-type legendary Pokemon. I also know this Pokemon only from the games but it was enough for me to like it! It has another forme as "Origin Forme" that is activated by giving it a Griseous Orb to hold or by using Griseous Core on it. The original forme is called "Altered Forme". Giratina is a member of the creation trio of Sinnoh (with Dialga and Palkia), representing antimatter.



Salamence is another good-looking and strong Pokemon from Generation-3. It is a dragon/flying pseudo-legendary Pokemon and it evolves from Shelgon starting at level 50. It is the final form of Bagon. Salamence can also mega evolve ino Mega Salamence using the Salamencite.



Tyranitar is another pseudo-legendary Pokemon from Generation-2. It is rock/dark-type Pokemon that looks like a dinosaur. It evolves from Pupitar that does not look like a strong Pokemon (even though Tyranitar is very powerful) starting at level 55. It can also mega evolve into Mega Tyranitar using the Tyranitarite.



Gyarados is another candidate to being a fan-favourite with its appearances in Generation-1. It was a big surprise for everyone when a little fish Pokemon Magikarp evolved into one of the strongest Pokemons of the Generation-1. It is a water/flying-type Pokemon with an ability to mega evolve using Gyaradosite.


This list was not in an order but the last Pokemon that I will mention is probably my favourite Pokemon among all the Pokemons. Charizard is another fan-favourite Pokemon that is a fire/flying-type Pokemon that looks and behaves like a dragon a lot. This strong Pokemon may not be a legendary but it is seen that it can even defeat a legendary Pokemon (Articuno). Charizard evolves from Charmeleon starting at level 36 and it is the final form of Charmander.
